Narrative Summary (100-150 words)

Payam Nahid, MD, MPH is a UCSF professor in the Department of Medicine and serves as Executive Director, Institute for Global Health Sciences. His recent portfolio centers on tuberculosis diagnostics, treatment optimization, and implementation science across high-burden settings. Over the past 12 months (2025-03-01 to 2026-03-01), UCSF profile data linked to PubMed shows sustained output in drug-resistant TB, diagnostic strategy, and trial design. Notable work includes near point-of-care TB screening impact and cost analyses, phase 3 trial evidence for resistant TB regimens, and studies that support people-centered diagnostic pathways. The overall pattern indicates translational leadership from evidence generation through implementation and policy-relevant application.
